Core Features

Everything your hotel needs, nothing it doesn't

🏨
Front Desk & Reservations
Check in and out guests, manage real-time room availability, and eliminate double bookings. Auto-syncs with Booking.com and Agoda.
🧹
Housekeeping Tracker
Assign rooms to staff through the app. Housekeepers get phone notifications, mark rooms as cleaned, and report damages — all logged automatically.
📱
QR Guest Requests
A simple QR code in each room lets guests message the front desk directly — no app download needed. Requests route to the right department automatically.
📊
Owner Dashboard
Daily occupancy rates, revenue summary, and pending issues — all visible in one screen. Know exactly how your property is performing, anytime.
💳
GCash & Maya Built-In
Accept local digital payments natively. No more manually verifying GCash screenshots — payments are confirmed and logged automatically.
🌐
Filipino Language Interface
Switch the entire interface between English and Filipino. Designed for the real staff running your hotel — not just tech-savvy managers.
